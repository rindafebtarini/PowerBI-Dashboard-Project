## üìå Project Overview
This project analyzes the operational business performance of foodpanda from August 2023 to August 2025. The dataset was obtained from the Kaggle website https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ayeshaimran1619/foodpanda-data-analysis/data. The dashboard provides deep insights into sales trends, customer profiles, and operational service quality to support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard is categorized into three main focus areas:
- Executive Overview: A summary of financial performance and key sales metrics.
- Customer Profile: Analysis of user demographics, purchasing behavior, and loyalty.
- Operational & Quality: Evaluation of delivery efficiency and customer satisfaction levels.

## üí° Key Metrics & Insights
1. Executive Performance
   - Total Revenue: Achieved $14.34M with a total quantity of 17,950 units sold.
   - Order Volume: Recorded 6,000 total orders with an Average Order Value (AOV) of $2.39K.
   - Market Leaders: Pizza Hut leads as the top-performing restaurant with $3.002M in sales, followed closely by Subway and KFC.
   - Top Category: The Italian food category is the largest contributor, generating $3.01M in total sales.
2. Customer Deep Dive
   - User Base: The platform services 6,000 total customers with a 50.3% active rate.
   - Demographics: Users are distributed across three age groups, with Teenagers (34.37%) representing the largest segment.
   - Regional Performance: Multan holds the largest user base with 1,256 users, followed by Lahore and Peshawar.
   - Payment Preferences: Cash is the most dominant payment method (2,039 transactions), followed by Card and Wallet options.
3. Operational Efficiency & Quality
   - Delivery Performance: The Delivery Success Rate stands at 34.33%, while the Cancellation Rate and Delay Rate are 32.80% and 32.87% respectively.
   - Product Quality: Sandwich products received the highest average rating of 3.04, whereas Pizza recorded the lowest at 2.92.
   - Satisfaction by Category: The Dessert category leads in customer satisfaction scores compared to other food categories.
